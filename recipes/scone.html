Scone

Description

Ingredients

  • flour
  • sugar
  • baking powder
  • butter
  • heavy cream
  • butter
  • egg

steps

  1. Mix the dry ingredients together
  2. Cut in the grated frozen butter
  3. Whisk the wet ingredients together
  4. Mix wet ingredients and dry ingredients
  5. Form into a disc and cut into wedges
  6. Brush with heavy cream or buttermilk
  7. Refrigerate for at least 15 minutes
  8. Bake until golden brown
